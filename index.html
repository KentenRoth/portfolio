About Me

I have been thinking about changing careers for a few years, and now I have started to teach myself how to code with courses on Udemy.com and Devslopes.com. I make time each night to do something that will help me advance towards the goal of becoming a developer. The thing that keeps me so interested in coding is the constant challenges and the amount of problem-solving that goes along with it. I also enjoy how many options of different languages and frameworks there are that you can learn. Coding was something that was very interesting to me in high school, but at that time my school only provided basic HTML and CSS class that didn't even last an entire semester. After high school, I enrolled at Hamilton College to learn computer science but was soon overwhelmed by how much it costs to live on my own. Due to financial issues, I had to drop out of college and go full time working. I started working as an automotive technician, and I have been with the same company for the last 14 years. During those 14 years, I got a lot better with managing my finances and enrolled at Kaplan University (now Purdue Global) and earned my Bachelor's Degree in Business Administration.

Projects

Projects in progress!

The Garden-App was built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The Garden-app is a simple app that will help you keep track of what you have planted in your garden and give you an estimated harvest time. When you plant seeds in your garden the seed package or the tag that comes with it should offer you an estimated harvest date. This app will allow you to track what plants you have planted, the day you planted, how many of each plant was planted, and give you an estimated harvest date based off of the information you provided.
